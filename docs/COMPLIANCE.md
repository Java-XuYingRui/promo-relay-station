# Compliance Notes

This toolkit is intended for lawful, user-owned promotional workflows.

## Allowed Uses

- Generate drafts from your own materials.
- Prepare publishing checklists and schedules.
- Create campaign assets for platforms where you have permission to post.
- Use official platform APIs when the platform provides them.
- Use browser-assisted publishing with human review and final confirmation.

## Disallowed Uses

- Bypassing platform login, CAPTCHA, QR scan, rate limits, or review systems.
- Posting spam, deceptive, infringing, fraudulent, or harassing content.
- Impersonating people, brands, or platforms.
- Publishing with accounts you do not own or operate.
- Uploading private credentials, API keys, session cookies, or payment data.
- Automating final publish actions where platform rules require human action.

## Recommended Review Checklist

- Confirm the account is authorized to publish the content.
- Confirm the target URL and claims are accurate.
- Avoid absolute claims such as "permanent", "unlimited", or "official" unless
  you can prove them.
- Keep credentials out of work item files, configs, logs, screenshots, and Git.
- Review each platform's current rules before posting.
